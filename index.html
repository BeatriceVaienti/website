About Me

I recently completed my PhD in Digital Humanities at the Digital Humanities Laboratory - DHLAB (EPFL). I hold a MSc in Building Engineering and Architecture. In my research I studied the Western cartographic depiction of Jerusalem between 1810 and 1925, focusing on errors and biases, and how they can be used to discover historical information.
